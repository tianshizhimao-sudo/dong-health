Trail Running Guide

Technique · Nutrition · Gear · Mental

Technique

⬆️ Uphill

Power hike with hands on thighs. This saves 40% energy vs running. Lean forward, drive with arms. Short steps.

⬇️ Downhill

Quick short steps, NOT big strides. Lean slightly forward. Knees never locked. Arms out for balance. This protects your quads.

🏃 Flat

Run easy (Zone 2). If you can't hold a conversation, you're going too fast. Save energy for the climbs.

🦵 Power Hike

The secret weapon. Fast walkers beat slow runners on trail. Practice it every Thursday session. Master it.

Nutrition Plan

⚡ Race Day Fuel

  • Pre-raceBreakfast 2hr before: oats or toast + coffee. Nothing new.
  • 0:45minFirst gel or 2 dates. Don't wait until you're hungry.
  • Every 45minGel (100cal) OR real food (banana, PB sandwich cut small)
  • Hydration500ml/hour. Drink at EVERY aid station even if not thirsty.
  • ElectrolytesSalt tab or electrolyte drink every 90min. No cramps.
  • Practice nowUse your race nutrition on ALL training runs from W4 onwards.

Mental Game

💬 When It Gets Hard

  • 📍"Next aid station" — break race into small segments, never think about the full distance
  • ⏳Slow down before you think you need to — don't blow up early
  • 🏔️Walking up is NOT failing — the best UTA runners power hike climbs strategically
  • 💪At 16km when the final climb starts: "I trained for exactly this moment"
  • 🎯Cutoff is 7 hours. Average pace to finish = 19 min/km. You have plenty of time.

Best Sydney Training Spots

📍 Recommended Routes

  • 🏙️Pyrmont Stairs — Quick and easy. Good for Thursday sessions. 5 min from CBD.
  • 🌊Bondi to Coogee — Undulating coastal path, 6km. Great for technique.
  • 🌿Ku-ring-gai Chase NP — 45 min drive. Actual trail, rocks, roots. Perfect W3-4.
  • 🏞️Royal National Park — 1hr south. Coastal Track is beautiful and hilly.
  • 🏔️Blue Mountains (Wentworth Falls) — 1.5hr west. BEST prep, closest to race terrain. Go W5+.

Recommended Shoes

👟 Trail Shoes — Get These ASAP

  • 🥇Salomon Speedcross 6 — Aggressive lugs for mud/rock. Most popular UTA shoe.
  • 🥈Hoka Speedgoat 5 — More cushion, great for first timer. Easier on quads downhill.
  • 🥉ASICS Gel-Trabuco — Budget option, still great grip.
  • ⚠️Road running shoes will slip on wet rocks. DO NOT race in road shoes.
  • 📅Buy by end of W2 and break them in for at least 4 weeks before race day.
